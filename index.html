About

My journey into the world of computer science officially began in 2021 when I started my degree. However, my true passion for video games and game development has been a driving force in my life since as early as 2020. Delving into the realms of coding and Unity, I've actively honed my skills and participated in various interdisciplinary projects, both independently and within teams.

My dedication led me to engage in renowned events such as the "Kenney's Jam" and the "Global Game Jam," where I thrived in the fast-paced and creative environments. Additionally, I successfully completed a Game Development Bootcamp with Generation, winning a scholarship amidst a competitive pool of over 2000 participants.

Proficient in Unity and well-versed in C#, I bring a solid foundation in game development. My experiences in game jams and bootcamps have equipped me with a diverse skill set and a deep passion for turning ideas into captivating interactive experiences. Now, I am eagerly seeking opportunities in the game industry to kickstart my career in the area that I love the most.
